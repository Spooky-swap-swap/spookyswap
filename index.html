What is SpookySwap?

SpookySwap is a decentralized exchange (DEX) and a comprehensive DeFi platform. It allows users to swap tokens, provide liquidity, and earn rewards through yield farming. As a part of the growing DeFi ecosystem, SpookySwap ensures seamless token swaps and liquidity management on multiple blockchains.

How to Use SpookySwap Bridge

Using the SpookySwap Bridge is simple and user-friendly:

  1. Visit: Go to the official website SpookySwap.
  2. Connect your wallet: Connect using MetaMask, WalletConnect, or any supported wallet.
  3. Select the asset and networks: Choose the token you want to bridge and specify the source and destination blockchains.
  4. Confirm and transfer: Review the transaction details, confirm, and transfer securely.
